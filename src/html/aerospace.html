AEROSPACE
DIVISION

Since 2008, our Aerospace division has provided one-stop metal solutions to support the growth of aviation and aerospace-related players. We are the sole distributor of Novelis, Constellium and UACJ aerospace components, and produce a wide range of materials including A2024, A6061, A7050, A7075 and Titanium.

Our facilities are ISO 9001:2015 certified and have received both AS9100 and AS9120 approval. As an environmentally-responsible company, we practice closed loop aluminium recycling as part of our sustainability efforts.

We specialise in docking systems, with capabilities that include:

  • Bushing
  • Anodising, Gallery, Latch, Edge Protector
  • Machining Aircraft Parts
  • Fastening Systems
  • Tooling
  • MRO
  • Docking System
  • Aircraft Materials
  • Aircraft Power Tool
  • Aircraft Tool
  • Aerospace Machine

Our core capabilities in other Aerospace-related sectors also encompass:

  • 2D/3D Modelling Design
  • CNC Gantry Plasma Cutting Services
  • Automated Band Saw Cutting Services
  • Shearing, Bending, Rolling & Fabrication Services
  • Surface & Side Milling Services
  • Precision & Rough Grinding Services
